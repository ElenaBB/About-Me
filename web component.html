Web Graphics

Please, see the result of my work in GIMP. This is the compilation of picturs of Tomsk and me in the center. Don't be puzzled by my gloomy look: the photo above clearely demonstartes minus 42 Celsius in December, and the ispiring slogan "Cheer up, people, the summer is getting on!". As for other pictures: the upper left is the main building of Tomsk State University, my Alma Mater. The bootom left represents Russian-German cultural house, this building is the former homestead of Tomsk merchant Golovanov. In the picture to the right you can see the monument to Anton Chekhov, a dramatist and author. The actual name for this extravagant monument is "Anton Pavlovich in Tomsk - drunkard's view, lying in a ditch, who never read Kashtanka". Leontiy Usov, the creator, was on the edge of being expelled from the Russian union of sculptors for such a modern view on Chekhov.
